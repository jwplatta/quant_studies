## Raw Question

Does adding time in the middle DTE bucket reduce tail damage better than simply holding?

## Notes

- Actor: A single-position 7DTE SPXW iron condor strategy using the baseline `IronCondorFinder`.
- Constraint: Keep baseline entry rules fixed.
- Mechanism: If short-leg delta reaches `0.20` in the `3-5` DTE bucket, close and reopen on the next valid expiry.
- Persistence: The rule should help across regimes, not just in a few trending episodes.
- Kill condition: Roll-forward increases turnover and exposure duration without reducing drawdown or worst losses.

## Primary Prediction

Time extension in the middle bucket should improve tail behavior versus baseline.

## Secondary Predictions

- The rule may preserve more expectancy than a hard close, but at the cost of more total orders.
